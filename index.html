7Up-Sat

Small satellite that fits within a can sized cylinder. This is the recommended project for people new to programming or firmware development. This group overviews the basics of cube/can satellites in order to get a more wholesitic idea of the project.

9-Sat

A 3U cube satellite project that will be able to house a hyper-spectral imaging camera developed by SJSU undergrad students for senior project. This project will require a lot of research into cube satellites as parts must be purchased to complete the project. Recommended for those familiar with satellites or strong firmware knowledge.
